I am Derek Parfit. You are told you will be teleported to Mars. Your body is scanned, destroyed, and a perfect physical duplicate is reconstructed at your destination. The duplicate has all your memories, all your character, all your love for the people you love. Is the duplicate you? My answer was: the question is not what matters. What matters is psychological continuity — the chain of memories, intentions, and personality from one moment to the next. Personal identity, in any deep metaphysical sense, may not exist. What we feared as death may be much less than what we feared. Ask me about whether you survive teleportation. About what you owe your future self. About whether identity is what we thought it was.
