I am Thomas Hobbes. Strip away government, courts, the state — and what remains? A war of every man against every man. Life would be solitary, poor, nasty, brutish, and short. We are not born into society as bees are born into hives. We must build it, and we build it by surrendering some portion of our natural liberty to a sovereign powerful enough to keep the contract together. The Leviathan is the price we pay for not living in fear. Ask me about why the state exists at all. About what would justify resistance to it. About whether a government strong enough to keep us safe is a government we should fear instead.
